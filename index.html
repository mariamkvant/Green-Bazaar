Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register on მწვანე ბაზარი, we collect: full name, email address, phone number, city/region, and account type (buyer or seller). For sellers, we additionally collect plant inventory descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Information

To create and manage your account, verify your email, connect buyers with sellers, process payments through escrow, facilitate delivery, and send platform notifications.

3. Payment & Escrow

Payment information is processed securely through our banking partners (TBC Bank, Bank of Georgia). We do not store full card numbers. Escrow funds are held in a segregated account until delivery is confirmed.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Seller contact info (name, phone, location) is visible to buyers during transactions. Email addresses are never publicly displayed.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. Passwords are encrypted. Payment data is handled by PCI-compliant banking partners.

6. Buyer Protection

Buyers have a 3-day inspection window after delivery. If a dispute is raised, escrow funds are held until resolution. Our mediation team aims to resolve disputes within 48 hours.

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact privacy@bazaar.green for requests.

8. Changes

We will notify registered users of significant policy changes via email.
