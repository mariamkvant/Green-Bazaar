Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register on მწვანე ბაზარი, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City/Region (location)
  • Account type (buyer or seller)

For sellers, we additionally collect information about the plants you grow and sell.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To verify your email address
  • To connect buyers with sellers
  • To display seller contact information on plant listings
  • To send important platform updates and notifications

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Seller contact information (name, phone, location) is visible to registered buyers when they inquire about a listing. Email addresses are never publicly displayed.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@bazaar.green

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.
