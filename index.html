Our Story

It all started when my father began growing Thuja plants in our garden in Georgia. What started as a hobby quickly turned into something bigger — neighbors wanted them, friends asked where to buy, and we realized there was no easy way for growers and buyers to connect.

That's how მწვანე ბაზარი was born. I wanted to create a space where anyone growing plants in Georgia could reach buyers directly — no middlemen, fair prices, and trust built into every transaction.

Today, we're building Georgia's first dedicated plant marketplace. Whether you're a small grower with Thuja saplings or a large nursery with hundreds of varieties, this is your platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and selling plants

How does the escrow payment work?

When you buy a plant, your payment is held securely by our platform — not sent directly to the seller. The seller only receives the money after you confirm the plant arrived in good condition. You have a 3-day inspection window after delivery.

What if my plant arrives damaged?

You can open a dispute within the 3-day inspection window. Upload photos of the damage, and our mediation team will review the case within 48 hours. You can request a full refund, partial refund, or replacement.

How do I start selling?

Every account can both buy and sell. Just click "+ Sell" in the navigation, fill in your plant details, add photos, and publish. Your listing will appear in the marketplace immediately.

What fees does the platform charge?

Buyers pay a 7% service fee on each purchase. This covers escrow protection, dispute resolution, and platform maintenance. Sellers receive the full listed price — no seller fees.

Can I sell seeds, fertilizers, or gardening tools?

Yes! მწვანე ბაზარი supports multiple categories: Plants, Seeds, Fertilizers, and Materials. You can list any gardening-related product.

How does delivery work?

You can choose between local pickup (free) or courier delivery (₾10). For local pickup, you and the seller coordinate a meeting point via in-app messaging. For courier delivery, the seller ships to your address.

How do I know if a seller is trustworthy?

Look for the "Verified Grower" badge — sellers earn this after 5+ successful orders with a 4.0+ star rating. You can also check their profile for reviews, ratings, and how long they've been on the platform.

What is the "Plant Arrived Healthy" badge?

When buyers confirm their plant arrived in good condition, it counts toward the seller's "Plant Arrived Healthy" percentage. This is displayed on listings so future buyers can see the seller's track record.

Can I negotiate the price?

Yes! Use the "Request This Plant" button to send a custom inquiry with your desired quantity and message. The seller can then respond with a custom quote or accept your request.

Is the platform available in Georgian?

Yes! Use the language selector in the navigation to switch between English, Georgian, and French.

What do Available, Limited Stock, and Pre-order mean?

Available — the plant is in stock and ready to ship immediately. Limited Stock — only a few left, buy soon before they're gone. Pre-order — the plant is seasonal or still growing. You pay now and the seller ships when it's ready (e.g., pomegranate trees for autumn planting).

How do I contact support?

Click your profile menu → Support, or scroll to the footer and click "Support". You can send a help request or partnership inquiry. We respond within 24 hours.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register on მწვანე ბაზარი, we collect: full name, email address, phone number, city/region, and account type (buyer or seller). For sellers, we additionally collect plant inventory descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Information

To create and manage your account, verify your email, connect buyers with sellers, process payments through escrow, facilitate delivery, and send platform notifications.

3. Payment & Escrow

Payment information is processed securely through our banking partners (TBC Bank, Bank of Georgia). We do not store full card numbers. Escrow funds are held in a segregated account until delivery is confirmed.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Seller contact info (name, phone, location) is visible to buyers during transactions. Email addresses are never publicly displayed.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. Passwords are encrypted. Payment data is handled by PCI-compliant banking partners.

6. Buyer Protection

Buyers have a 3-day inspection window after delivery. If a dispute is raised, escrow funds are held until resolution. Our mediation team aims to resolve disputes within 48 hours.

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact privacy@bazaar.green for requests.

8. Changes

We will notify registered users of significant policy changes via email.
